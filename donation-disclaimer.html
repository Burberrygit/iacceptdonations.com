Donation Disclaimer

The donation is the product. The product is nothing.

Refund requests may be handled through Stripe or PayPal according to their policies and transaction tools. If a refund is needed, use the receipt or payment-provider record associated with your donation.

Unless stated otherwise, iAcceptDonations.com is not presented as a registered charity, and donations should not be treated as tax-deductible charitable contributions.
